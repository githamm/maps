The map shows pending and approved permits for oil and gas wells (and schools , which will show up when zoomed in). Map markers are clustered and show the number of permits; click a cluster to zoom. Click a marker for details, including a link to public comments and other information on the Colorado Oil and Gas Conservation Commission's website, and a link to view the location in a Google map*. To toggle between pending and approved permits, or change to a satellite background, click the map layers icon in the upper right corner of the map. Use the search bar to zoom to an address. The map will be updated weekly.

*A useful feature in Google maps is the ability to measure the distance between two points: Right-click on one point, choose "Measure distance" then click the other point.
