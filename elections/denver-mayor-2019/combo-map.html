Mayoral election results by precinct

Mayor Michael Hancock got the most votes Denver's May 7 election, but it wasn't enough to avoid a runoff. He and the second-place finisher, Jamie Giellis, will meet again on June 4. Hancock took first-place in most of the city's precincts, but Giellis took territory in east and south Denver.
